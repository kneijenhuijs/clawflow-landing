Common questions

"I could build this myself."
Absolutely. That's exactly what I did — it took about 30 hours to get right. The cron orchestration, the memory search integration, the multi-project architecture, the edge cases. ClawFlow saves you that time. You're buying proven workflows, not just code.
What do I need to run ClawFlow?
OpenClaw (the free version works fine). Optionally: a Todoist account and Google Calendar for integrations. The free skill runs on any OpenClaw setup. The Complete template works best with the OpenClaw cron system.
Is this a subscription?
No. $99 once. You own it forever. Future updates included. No recurring fees, no "pro tier," no bait-and-switch.
Is my data private?
100%. Everything runs locally on your machine through OpenClaw. No data leaves your computer. No cloud, no analytics, no tracking. Your notes, your summaries, your memory — all yours.
Can I customize the schedule and format?
Yes. Change cron times, modify brief templates, add integrations, adjust the output format. The Complete template includes a full customization playbook that walks you through everything.
What if it doesn't work for me?
Try the free skill first — it gives you a taste of the system with no risk. If the Complete template doesn't meet your expectations, reach out. I want this to work for you.
What if I use ClickUp / Monday / Notion?
Every paid template includes 1 custom integration setup via GitHub issue. We configure it async — no call needed.
Can I use multiple task managers at once?
Yes! Use Todoist + Linear together. ClawFlow merges them in your briefs.
